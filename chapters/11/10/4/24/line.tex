        \vec{A} = \frac{1}{17}\myvec{-1\\22}
    \end{align*}

    $\vec{A}$ to reach it in the shortest time. The normal vector 
    of \eqref{eq:chapters/11/10/4/24/L3} is 
    \begin{align}
         \myvec{6\\-7}
	 \implies
        \vec{n} = \myvec{7\\6}
        \label{eq:chapters/11/10/4/24/L4-norm}
    \end{align}
